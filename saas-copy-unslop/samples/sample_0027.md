**FAQ Section Copy**

**What does your payroll platform do?**  
Our platform helps startups run payroll for global employees and contractors in one place. You can manage onboarding, collect local payroll details, automate pay runs, track compliance tasks, and get clear visibility into total workforce costs.

**Who is this for?**  
It is designed for startups and scaling companies hiring across borders. If you have remote employees, international contractors, or plan to expand into new countries, the platform is built to reduce the operational overhead that usually comes with global payroll.

**Can I pay both employees and contractors?**  
Yes. You can manage both full-time employees and contractors from the same system, with workflows tailored to each type of worker. That means fewer spreadsheets, fewer disconnected tools, and less manual follow-up.

**How many countries do you support?**  
We support global teams across a wide range of countries, with coverage designed around where modern startups hire. If you are evaluating specific markets, our team can confirm support and setup requirements for each one.

**How does compliance work?**  
We help you stay aligned with local payroll requirements by guiding setup, automating key workflows, and surfacing country-specific requirements before payroll is processed. This reduces the risk of missed filings, incorrect calculations, and late payments.

**How long does setup take?**  
Most startups can get up and running quickly, especially if they are moving from manual processes or disconnected local providers. Setup time depends on team size, countries involved, and whether you are onboarding employees, contractors, or both.

**Can you handle fast-growing teams?**  
Yes. The platform is built for startups that plan to hire quickly across multiple regions. Whether you are adding your first international contractor or building teams in several countries at once, the system scales with you.

**What makes this different from traditional payroll providers?**  
Traditional payroll providers are often built for large enterprises or single-country operations. We focus on what startups actually need: faster onboarding, simpler workflows, global visibility, and less operational drag on finance and people teams.

**Do I need local entities in every country?**  
Not always. The answer depends on how you hire and where your team is based. We help you understand your options and choose the right setup for each country and worker type.

**Will my finance team get reporting and payroll visibility?**  
Yes. You get a centralized view of payroll costs, upcoming pay runs, team spend, and payment status across countries. This gives finance leaders cleaner reporting and better forecasting without stitching data together manually.

**Is pricing transparent?**  
Yes. Startups need predictable costs, so pricing is designed to be clear and easy to understand. You can see what you will pay for payroll operations before committing.

**Can you help us switch from spreadsheets or another provider?**  
Yes. We support teams migrating from manual payroll workflows, local vendors, and fragmented tools. Our onboarding process is designed to reduce disruption and help you transition cleanly.
